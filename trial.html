ABOUT

BAY AREA'S BIGGEST BEGINNER-ORIENTED HACKATHON

Script & Style Hacks is a 24-hour, beginner-oriented hackathon that is open to all middle school and high school students of all levels. Its goal is to create a safe and inclusive environment for young students to learn technical skills and promote gender equality in tech by establishing a near 50-50 ratio of female and male attendees. Attendees have the opportunity to attend workshops and develop any project of their interest from scratch under the guidance of industry professionals and mentors throughout the entire event. At the end of the event there will be a judging period and students will win fun prizes for their hard work and creativity that is shown through their project. Script & Style Hacks is dedicated to pushing the mission of Girls Who Code Inc. forward - closing the gender gap in tech and promoting Girls Who Code's core values - bravery, girls-first leadership, quality and candor.
